Savills India has been Great Place to Work® certified in India from February 2024 to February 2025. This recognition marks the company's fifth accreditation within its first five years of full-fledged operations in India. The certification recognises Savills India’s commitment to building a diverse and inclusive culture through initiatives like WoVangelist aimed at promoting wellbeing for women at workplace. Another program, Mentor Moms, connects new mothers with mentors as they transition back to work after maternity leave. Additionally, our annual rewards and recognition program, Savills Spotlight, is designed to shine a light on the unwavering dedication, integrity, and potential of our exceptional employees.
